Our Mission

A Benedictine Tradition of Beauty

Founded in 1895 by monks from Saint John's Abbey in Minnesota, Saint Martin's Abbey follows the Rule of St. Benedict — a way of life built on community, hospitality, and the dignity of work. Since 1980 the Abbey Events series has brought the world's finest chamber musicians and scholars to Lacey, Washington. Every event is an act of welcome: open doors, open hearts, no charge at the door.
