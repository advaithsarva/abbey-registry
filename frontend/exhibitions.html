Since 1980, Saint Martin's Abbey has presented over 150 scholarly lectures and live performances in the Abbey — always free and open to the public.

The Venue

The Abbey

Completed in 1971 and designed by Bennett, Johnson & Associates of Olympia, the Abbey is an octagonal worship space built of brick and western red cedar. Flexible seating surrounds a moveable altar, making it ideal for both liturgy and live performance. At its heart sits a Steinway Concert Grand Piano.
