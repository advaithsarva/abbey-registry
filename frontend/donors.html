An Offering of Hospitality

Sustaining a Sacred Tradition

Founded in 1895, Saint Martin's Abbey has welcomed guests in the spirit of St. Benedict for over a century. The Abbey Events series — launched in 1980 — has offered more than 150 performances and lectures with never a ticket price. The series endures through a small endowment, freewill offerings, and the faithfulness of those who believe beauty belongs to everyone.
