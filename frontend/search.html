NLP-Powered Search

Search by Meaning

Type naturally. Our engine scores artworks by how well they match your words — title, artist, medium, and description.

How the Search Works

✍️

1. You Type

Enter any natural language query — a mood, theme, artist, or medium.

🔤

2. Tokenise

We split your query into keywords and remove filler words like "the", "by", "a".

📊

3. Score

Each keyword is matched against Title (+3), Artist (+2), Medium (+2), Description (+1).

🏆

4. Rank

Artworks are sorted by total score. Highest score appears first.

Scoring System

How Points are Awarded

Field MatchPointsWhy
Title+3Most specific — title directly names the work
Artist Name+2Searching by creator is very intentional
Medium+2e.g. "oil painting" is a specific constraint
Description+1Broader context — least specific match
